Introduction

Owen's Pizza

Owen's pizza proudly serves the highest quality pizza in all of San Luis Obispo. Our founder, Owen Christensen, always dreamt of a world where the finest quality pizza brought his community together.

Target audience

The people who use this site are those who live in or are visiting San Luis Obispo looking for a fun, easy, delicious option for a meal.

Visitors are visiting the site to get a sense of the restaraunt's environment, reliability, quality, and pricing options. Their goal is to decide if this restaraunt will be a good palce to visit for them at the moment or sometime in the future.

About

Our founder, Owen Christensen, was tired of the average pizza place. Owen dreamed of a place that ensured quality, affordable pizza, and a good time all at once. It didn't take long for Owen to get to work. In 2022, he single-handedly built Owen's Pizza with his bare hands. Since then, Owen's Pizza has held the unofficial title of Best Pizza in SLO!
